Our Story

A Legacy of Passion
& Flavour

"Cooking is not just about nourishment. It is about bringing people together, one extraordinary dish at a time."

— Chef Jean-Pierre Laurent, Founder

Founded in Paris in 1987 by Chef Jean-Pierre Laurent, Maison Laurent began as a small neighbourhood bistro with a single vision: to elevate everyday ingredients into extraordinary experiences.

Today, our three-Michelin-starred restaurant welcomes guests in Paris, New York, and Tokyo — each location carrying the same soul, the same obsession with quality, and the same warm hospitality that made us who we are.

Careers at Maison Laurent

We invest in every member of our kitchen and floor teams, and we take pride in the consistency that has earned us three Michelin stars. Below is an excerpt from our internal Staff Handbook and Brand Guidelines — the standards every new team member learns from day one.

📋

Staff Handbook (excerpt)

Essential standards reviewed in every new team member's first week, ensuring every guest receives the same exceptional experience at every table, every service.

  • Service sequence: greet every guest within 30 seconds, present menus from the right, and follow our seven-step tableside service standard.
  • Grooming & uniform: pressed uniforms, polished shoes, and minimal fragrance — presentation reflects the kitchen's precision.
  • Scheduling & shift protocols: arrive 15 minutes before service for briefing, with a full handover of table notes between shifts.
  • Role responsibilities: documented mise en place, service, and closing checklists for both kitchen and floor teams.
  • Career progression: a clear path from commis to chef de partie, or server to captain, with quarterly reviews and senior mentorship.
🎨

Brand Guidelines (excerpt)

A consistent voice and visual identity across every touchpoint — from the menu to social media to the way we greet a guest on the phone.

  • Logo & visual identity: approved usage of the Maison Laurent crest, script, and serif marks across print, signage, and digital.
  • Color & typography system: our signature gold and ivory palette, paired with Playfair Display for headings and Inter for body text.
  • Brand voice: warm, refined, and attentive — written communication favors elegant simplicity over embellishment.
  • Marketing templates: approved layouts for seasonal menus, event invitations, and social media posts.
  • Guest correspondence scripts: standard responses for reservation confirmations, special requests, and review replies.

Interested in joining the Maison Laurent family? We're always looking for passionate people for our kitchen and floor teams.
